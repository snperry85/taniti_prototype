Getting Around

Public Bus

Public buses serve Taniti City and run daily from 5:00 a.m. to 11:00 p.m. This is the most affordable way to get around the city. Private buses serve the rest of the island.

Taxi

Taxis are available throughout Taniti City and can be hailed on the street or arranged through your hotel. Taxis are a convenient option for late-night travel after buses stop running.

Rental Car

Rental cars are available from a local agency located near the airport. A rental car is ideal if you plan to explore beyond Taniti City. A valid driver's license is required.

Bike Rental

Bikes and helmets are available to rent from several vendors around the island. Helmets are required by law. Taniti City is fairly flat and very bikeable for visitors of all ages.

Walking

Taniti City and the Merriton Landing area are both easy to explore on foot. Most hotels, restaurants, and attractions in these areas are within comfortable walking distance of each other.

Cruise Ship Shuttle

Visitors arriving by cruise ship dock at Yellow Leaf Bay. Hotel shuttles and taxis are available at the dock to take you into Taniti City and the surrounding areas.
