Places to Stay

Four-Star Resort

Taniti's one large four-star resort offers full amenities, including pools, restaurants, and beach access. Ideal for families and travelers seeking a premium experience. Suitable for large parties. Located near Yellow Leaf Bay.

Family Hotels

A wide selection of small, family-owned hotels is available throughout Taniti City and the surrounding areas. These offer comfortable, affordable accommodation with a personal touch. Good for families of all sizes.

Bed & Breakfast

A growing number of bed and breakfasts offer a cozy, home-style stay with breakfast included. Best suited for couples or small families of up to 4 guests.

Hostel

Taniti's inexpensive hostel is a great option for budget travelers and solo visitors. Shared and private room options are available. Best for individuals or pairs. Located within walking distance of Taniti City.

Private Condo / Home

Private condos and vacation homes are available for rent, offering more space and privacy for larger groups or longer stays. Many include kitchen facilities. Ideal for families of 5 or more.

All lodging on Taniti is strictly regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government.
